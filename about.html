Rebecca C. Cory, Ph.D., is a lifelong college educator and collaborative feminist leader who has focused on educational equity across student and academic affairs since 1994.

Most recently the Dean of Curriculum and Assessment at Bellevue College, she has coedited two books on accessible pedagogy including Universal Design in Higher Education, from Principles to Practice (Harvard Education Press). Dr. Cory has served as full-time and sessional faculty at six institutions. As an administrator, she uses collaborative inquiry to support faculty and the wider college community in their shared pursuit of social justice.

She relaxes year-round by swimming in the waters of Puget Sound and Lake Washington.
