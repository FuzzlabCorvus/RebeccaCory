Rebecca creates individualized service packages tailored to each family’s needs. Typically, coaching begins with weekly one-on-one sessions with the student and transitions to biweekly meetings as skills and independence develop. Packages include monthly family meetings and ongoing email or phone support. Services start at $3,000 and are priced based on complexity and duration of support.

Rebecca can attend an IEP meeting for an additional fee. Her goal is to support the student in running their own IEP meeting, and advocating for their needs appropriately in that setting.

Rebecca also can help a student navigate disciplinary situations in high school or college. If you would like an experienced educator to see you through the disciplinary system, and don’t want the adversarial action of hiring a lawyer, her services may be for you. Contact her for rates and scope of work.
