Do you feel disorganized, miss deadlines, or believe your grades don’t reflect your abilities? You may benefit from working with Rebecca. She understands how frustrating it can be to feel capable yet overwhelmed, and she helps students recognize that challenges often stem from mismatches between learning environments and individual needs; not personal failure.
Through coaching, students learn practical organization strategies, effective communication skills, and how to advocate for their academic and social needs. The goal is greater confidence, reduced conflict at home, and improved success in school and beyond.
